📘 StratForge Arena — Manual

Getting Started

1. Get a Deriv API token from app.deriv.com → Account Settings → API Token. Use Read + Trade scopes.

2. Paste your token in the Connection box and click Connect.

3. Select your Market, Mode, and Style Preset.

4. Click Start Ticks to begin data collection, then Start Bot when the Analyzer is ready.

Trade Modes

📈 Rise / Fall — Predict whether the next price will be higher (Rise) or lower (Fall). Three strategies:

  • Balanced Play — EMA 10/20 crossover. Waits for trend confirmation before entering.
  • Quick Fire — Bollinger Band breakout. Fast entries with Martingale 2x recovery.
  • Calm Mode — Requires 3 consecutive ticks in same direction. Anti-martingale, strict loss limit.

🔢 Over / Under — Bet on whether the last digit of the price is above or below a number (usually 4 or 5).

  • Balanced Play — Over 4 · Enters when 50-tick histogram shows ≥60% high digits (5–9).
  • Quick Fire — Over 4 · Strong bias ≥65%, Martingale 1.8x.
  • Calm Mode — Under 5 · Low digit dominance ≥58%, PLS recovery (1.3x only).

🎯 Digits Lab — Even/Odd bias detection across 50, 100, and 200-tick rolling windows.

  • Balanced Play — Both 50-tick and 100-tick windows must confirm bias before trading.
  • Quick Fire — 50-tick only. Fast entries, Martingale 2x after each loss.
  • Calm Mode — 100+200 tick confirmation. Strictest thresholds, lowest stake, most cooldown.

Style Presets

Presets configure stake size, staking mode, risk controls, and signal filters all at once.

  • Balanced Play — Fixed stake. Medium speed. Reasonable risk. Good starting point.
  • Quick Fire — Martingale or fast PLS recovery. Highest potential reward AND risk. Requires larger account.
  • Calm Mode — Anti-martingale or gentle PLS. Smallest stakes. Strict loss streak cutoff. Best for protecting capital.

XP & Levels

You earn XP for every completed trade (25 XP for wins, 5 XP for losses). Reach higher levels to unlock profile titles:

  • Level 1: Rookie (0 XP)
  • Level 2: Analyst (500 XP)
  • Level 3: Sniper (1,500 XP)
  • Level 4: Strategist (3,000 XP)
  • Level 5: Mastermind (6,000 XP)
  • Level 6: Phantom (12,000 XP)
  • Level 7: Legend (25,000 XP)

Accumulator Dashboard

📊 Accumulator adds a dashboard lane for Overseer supervision. It lets Overseer tune trend-bias, ladder step %, target %, and watch-mode pacing while you decide when to escalate to live routing.
